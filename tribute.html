Sundar Pichai

CEO of Google

Here's a time line of Sundar's life:

  • 1972 - Born in Chennai, south-east India.
  • 2004 - Joined google as product manager and innovation efforts leading Google Chrome and Chrome OS
  • 2009 - Pichai gave a demonstration of Chrome OS.
  • 2011 - has released the Chromebook for trial and testing.
  • 2012 - has released chromebook to the public.
  • 2010 - has announced the open-sourcing of the new video codec VP8 by Google and introduced the new video format,WebM.
  • 2013 - hasadded Android to the list of Google products that he oversees.
  • 2015 - was selected to become the next CEO of Google after previously being appointed Product Chief by CEO, Larry Page.
  • 2016 - he stepped into the new position at the completion of the formation of Alphabet Inc., the new holding company for the Google company family.
  • 2017 - Pichai drew publicity for firing a Google employee who wrote a ten-page manifesto criticizing the company's diversity policies
